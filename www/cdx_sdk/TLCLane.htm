TLC Lane Object

CDXML Name:tlclane
CDX Constant Name:kCDXObj_TLCLane
CDX Constant Value:0x8024
Contained by objects:kCDXObj_TLCPlate
First written/read in:ChemDraw 8.0

Description:

TLC Lane objects technically have no required properties or objects, but should probably contain at least one Spot if they are to be useful.

A single spot on a TLC plate.
